Welcome to Contact Answer!

Contact Answer is an Answering Services provider for businesses needs to be met via our call center.

What is an Answering Service?
An Answering Service is exactly as it sounds, A person or entity that will take calls on your behalf, the main difference being that we provide it as a service. Here at CONTACT AUTOMATION we believe that each customer should be attended to personally and professionally, which is why we do what we love to do. Reaching out or instilling in customers your brands attitude on customer service is everything from lead generation to dispersement of information.
Types of Answering Services provided?
While there are various types of Answering Services we provide, Our development process is completely customizable. From the script to the toll free number, we can help you gain an understanding of our hybrid model of answering services and why it's essential to what you need. First we have Live Answering Services. These allow us to speak personally to your customers without long wait times and then document what can or needs to be done. This is one of our best services as it allows businesses to build to scale and utilize our services instead of having their own call center. With this we can prioritize or document various methods of engagement and let you know exactly what the customer is looking for. Secondly, we have Virtual Receptionist Services. This allows our agents to be used for your benefit specifically. We will build a script specialized to you, train an agent(or a few!) and have a purpose built initiative for reaching out to you on ONLY what you would like. We also host specialty services for when businesses have to take donations or charities need more manpower than they're capable of hiring. We can host those lines and allow your needs to be met by us, or simply utilize our agents and your own staff. You may be asking yourself why this matters, and honestly, it matters because of the principle of connection. If your business can't take a call or has to much calls for the time of day, you get bogged down in customer interaction and not your business. This is a perfect reason why Answering Services is viable for you. Let us handle the customer, you handle the business, and everyone benefits.
